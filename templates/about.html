BIT-z-BOTS mission

home makestation gallery about

Bit-z-Bots, a web application for girls to customize and create their own miniature 3D-printed robot figurine, is a tribute to Anne’s childhood spent borrowing her brother’s Legos and science kits.

Cute and unassuming, a Bit-z-Bot’s noble purpose in its 3D-fabricated life is to foster interest in science and technology while combating the lack of creative, intelligent, tech-related toys available for girls.

Why give a girl a doll if she can make her own?
